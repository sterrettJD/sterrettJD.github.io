I'm a graduate student in the Behavioral Neuroendocrinology Lab within the Integrative Physiology department at the University of Colorado at Boulder. I study gut microbiota community structure and diet-microbiome interactions in the context of mood affective and stressor-related disorders. I'm particularly interested in developing multi-omics approaches to characterizing changes microbial communities and the immune system in response to psychosocial stress.
